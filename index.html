Your privacy is important to us. The Application is designed to be a completely self-contained experience on your device.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or any data from your device.

All game data, such as your progress, settings, or statistics, is stored locally on your device only and is never accessed by or transmitted to Power9Proxy or any third party.
